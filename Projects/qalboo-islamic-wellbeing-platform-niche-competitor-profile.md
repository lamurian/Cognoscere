## Summary

Qalboo is an Islamic wellbeing platform founded in 2022 in Jakarta by Arjun Sumarlan and Windy Dhaliana. The platform integrates modern clinical psychology approaches with Islamic values from the Quran and Sunnah, offering counseling and mindfulness tailored to Muslim users. It is backed by Antler and Iterative [@tracxn2026; @qalboo2024].

## Key Points

**User Base.** Qalboo has not publicly disclosed user numbers. As a 2022 startup with pre-seed funding only, it is in early growth stage. Its Tracxn score is 31/100 [@tracxn2026].

**Funding.** Qalboo raised an undisclosed pre-seed round from Antler and Iterative. The exact amount has not been made public, but typical Antler pre-seed rounds in Southeast Asia range from $100K to $500K [@tracxn2026].

**Product.** Qalboo provides Islamic-integrated mental health services combining: (1) Clinical psychology approaches with Islamic values; (2) Mindfulness and mental wellness content from an Islamic perspective; (3) Counseling services. The app is designed for Muslims seeking mental health support that aligns with their faith. Key differentiator: faith-based approach, which competitor apps (Riliv, KALM, Bicarakan) do not explicitly offer [@qalboo2024].

**Go-to-Market Strategy.** Qalboo identified an underserved niche: Indonesian Muslims (87% of the population) who want mental health care that respects Islamic values. The GTM leverages this niche positioning rather than competing directly with general-purpose apps. Backing from Antler and Iterative provides credibility and network effects.

**Current Marketing Strategy.** Qalboo's marketing is faith-community focused. It positions itself as an Islamic wellbeing platform on app stores and social media. The company likely leverages mosque networks, Islamic organizations, and faith-based communities for distribution. As an early-stage startup, its marketing spend is minimal.

**Competitive Position.** Qalboo holds a unique niche position with no direct Islamic-wellbeing competitors in Indonesia. However, the niche also limits total addressable market. Tracxn score 31/100 ties it with KALM [@tracxn2026].
