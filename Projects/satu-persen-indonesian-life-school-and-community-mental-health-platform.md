## Summary

Satu Persen positions itself as 'the largest life-school platform in Indonesia,' focusing on mental health education, self-development, and life skills rather than clinical counseling. Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Jakarta with offices in Bandung and Yogyakarta, Satu Persen takes a content-first, community-driven approach to mental health [@tracxn2026].

## Key Points

**User Base.** Satu Persen has a massive content-driven audience. Its YouTube channel has 3.63 million subscribers and 264 million total video views as of July 2026, with approximately 7.09 million views in the last 30 days [@vidiq2026]. The website claims '20,000+ others' in its community, though this appears to refer to its paid community/learning platform rather than content reach [@ptsatupersenedukasi2024]. The Satu Persen Podcast has reached Top 10 Podcasts in Indonesia on Spotify and Apple Podcasts.

**Funding.** Satu Persen is classified as unfunded by Tracxn with a score of 35/100. It has not raised institutional VC funding, instead growing organically through content monetization, paid workshops, coaching, psychological testing (B2B), and community programs [@tracxn2026].

**Product Offerings.** Satu Persen offers a wide range of services beyond typical mental health apps: (1) Life Coaching (individual, teen, couple, child); (2) Counseling; (3) Financial Planning; (4) Workshops, Webinars, Bootcamps, Online Classes; (5) Mental Health Training for Companies; (6) Psychological Testing (B2B for corporate hiring/assessment); (7) Gift Cards; (8) YouTube Membership; (9) Free community learning groups across Jakarta, Bandung, and Yogyakarta. It also has a sister company, Yoga The Life Society [@ptsatupersenedukasi2024].

**Go-to-Market Strategy.** Satu Persen's GTM is radically different from clinical counseling apps. It launched as a media/education platform first (YouTube content, podcast, blog), building a massive organic audience before monetizing through paid services. This content-first approach required minimal upfront capital -- no funding needed because content creation is low-cost relative to clinical service delivery. The company partners with universities, corporations, and government agencies for workshops and training.

**Current Marketing Strategy.** Satu Persen's marketing engine is its content flywheel: (1) YouTube channel with 3.63M subscribers producing regular psychology and self-development content; (2) Podcast in Top 10 nationally; (3) Instagram with strong engagement through relatable infographics and reels; (4) Community ambassador programs (Satu Persen Ambassador); (5) Collaboration with brands like blu by BCA Digital for sponsored events; (6) B2B partnerships for corporate training and psychological assessment services. The company actively pursues community collaborations (school OSIS, campus organizations, corporate, government) [@ptsatupersenedukasi2024].

**Competitive Position.** Tracxn score 35/100, ranked 3rd behind Riliv (52) and Teduh (37) [@tracxn2026]. However, by audience reach (3.63M YouTube subscribers vs Riliv's 386K TikTok followers), Satu Persen has far greater organic content distribution. Its model is less directly competitive with clinical counseling apps and more complementary -- it focuses on the education/prevention layer while competitors focus on treatment/counseling.
