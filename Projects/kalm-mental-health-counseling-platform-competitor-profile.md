## Summary

KALM (PT Sanubari Senantiasa Sejahtera) is a Jakarta-based online and offline counseling platform founded in 2017. It positions itself as an affordable, accessible mental health service with flexible counseling options and community-building events [@ptsanubarisenantiasasejahtera2026].

## Key Points

**User Base.** KALM claims over 50,000 people have used the platform, as displayed on its website with the tagline '50.000+ orang sudah bercerita' [@ptsanubarisenantiasasejahtera2026]. This is significantly lower than Riliv's 900,000+ downloads. KALM has not publicly disclosed funding rounds and is classified as unfunded by Tracxn, with a score of 31/100 [@tracxn2026].

**Product Features.** KALM offers four core services: (1) Ongoing Chat Counseling -- a multi-day chat-based therapy model where clients can message counselors over several days; (2) Video Counseling; (3) Offline Counseling (in-person sessions); (4) Gratitude Journal and Wellbeing Tracker for self-management. The platform also hosts KALM Talks, paid educational webinars on mental health topics priced at Rp50,000-62,500 per session [@ptsanubarisenantiasasejahtera2026].

**Pricing.** KALM's base pricing is Rp33,000 per day for ongoing chat counseling, positioning it as a mid-range option. This installment-based model makes it more affordable upfront than single-session pricing from competitors [@ptsanubarisenantiasasejahtera2026].

**Go-to-Market Strategy.** KALM launched as a B2C-focused platform and later introduced KALM for Business, a corporate mental health program. Unlike Riliv's full-stack approach (counseling + meditation + journaling), KALM concentrates on counseling as its core value proposition. The company uses community events (KALM Talks, live webinars) as lead generation and brand awareness tools [@ptsanubarisenantiasasejahtera2026].

**Current Marketing Strategy.** KALM relies on organic discovery through Google Play Store, its educational blog with psychology articles covering relationships, personality, and mental health topics, and hosted events. The company maintains a referral program and social media presence but has not achieved the viral TikTok engagement that Riliv has. KALM is mentioned in academic literature as one of several well-established DMHS providers in Indonesia [@anddrewrichmondthezo2024].

**Competitive Position.** KALM scores 31/100 on Tracxn, tied with Qalboo and behind Riliv (52), Teduh (37), and Satu Persen (35). Its key differentiator is the ongoing chat counseling model (multi-day format) and the Gratitude Journal plus Wellbeing Tracker feature set [@tracxn2026].
