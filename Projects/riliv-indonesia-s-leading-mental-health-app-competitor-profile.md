## Summary

Riliv is Indonesia's top-ranked mental health app by Tracxn score (52/100), founded in 2015 in Surabaya by Audrey Maximillian Herli and his brother Audy Christopher Herli. It started as a campus counseling service at Airlangga University, then evolved into a full-stack mental health platform offering online counseling, meditation, journaling, and sleep-aid features [@eastventures2023].

## Key Points

**User Base.** As of March 2023, Riliv had over 900,000 downloads across Indonesia. The company reported growing from 1,000 initial users at launch in 2015 to over 150,000 users by end of 2015, with steady growth thereafter [@eastventures2023; @anddrewrichmondthezo2024]. The app has an average rating of 4.7 and partners with over 100 licensed psychologists. Top penetration cities are West Java, DKI Jakarta, East Java, and Central Java. As of 2026, the company has 69 employees [@tracxn2026].

**Funding.** Riliv raised a seed round in January 2022 led by East Ventures, with participation from Benson Capital, Sankalpa Ventures, Teja Ventures, Telkom Indigo, and angel investor Shweta Shrivastava. The seed round was approximately $2.66M [@tracxn2026]. The company is now classified as a public entity per Tracxn [@intanrakhmayantidewi2022].

**Go-to-Market Strategy.** Riliv's initial GTM was campus-first: founder Maxi launched free counseling services for fellow students at Airlangga University, then joined a local incubator program hosted by the Surabaya city government. This grassroots approach validated demand before product development. Post-incubator, Riliv adopted a dual B2B+B2C model simultaneously [@stacyoentoro2022]. The B2C channel offers low-cost individual counseling at Rp100,000/session (significantly cheaper than competitors like Ibunda.id at Rp179,000+). The B2B channel (Riliv for Company/Business) provides Employee Assistance Programs including mental health tests, psychologist consultations, financial/nutritional consultations, webinars, and corporate training [@pamelavaladez2026].

**Current Growth Strategy.** Riliv's current marketing strategy relies heavily on social media, particularly TikTok where it has 386,600 followers and 12.8 million likes, making it the most-followed Indonesian mental health app on the platform [@anddrewrichmondthezo2024]. B2B corporate partnerships are a major growth driver, with Riliv for Company targeting enterprises needing employee mental health support. The company also does content marketing through educational articles, meditation guides, and sleep stories. Riliv expanded services to include Plus Counseling, Couple Counseling, Instant Counseling, and Offline Counseling. The company emphasizes government alignment, collaborating with Puskesmas and public health initiatives to destigmatize mental health care [@eastventures2023].

**Competitive Position.** Ranked 1st among 13 active competitors by Tracxn. It competes with Teduh (Tracxn score 37), Satu Persen (35), Psikologimu (35), Qalboo (31), KALM (31), and Tenang AI (30) [@tracxn2026].
