## Summary

Tenang AI is a Jakarta-based AI mental health startup founded in 2022 by Salma Dias Saraswati and Achmad Ricky Budianto. It operates an empathetic AI co-psychologist named Nesya, designed specifically for Millennials, Gen Z, and Gen Alpha in Indonesia. The company has raised $300K in seed funding [@tracxn2026; @tenangai2024].

## Key Points

**User Base.** Tenang AI has not publicly disclosed user numbers. As a seed-stage 2022 startup with $300K raised, it is in very early growth stage. Its Tracxn score is 30/100, ranked 7th among Riliv's competitors [@tracxn2026].

**Funding.** Tenang AI raised $300K in seed funding. Investors include participants from the Founder Institute and other angel investors. The latest funding round was in March 2024 [@tracxn2026].

**Product.** Tenang AI introduces Nesya, an AI-powered mental health companion designed specifically for Indonesian Gen Z and Millennials. Key differentiators: (1) AI-first model -- no human psychologist matching required for initial triage; (2) Focus on low-to-medium daily stress rather than clinical disorders; (3) Empathetic AI co-psychologist approach, reducing stigma by removing the human intermediary. The platform connects users to online therapy services for escalation when needed [@tenangai2024].

**Go-to-Market Strategy.** Tenang AI's GTM leverages the AI hype cycle and low-cost scalability. By deploying an AI chatbot as the primary interface, the company avoids the supply constraint that limits human-counseling platforms (shortage of licensed psychologists in Indonesia). The MIT Solve challenge participation provides credibility, and the TikTok-ready branding (Gen Z focused, casual tone) targets the same demographic that Riliv reaches through TikTok [@tenangai2024].

**Current Marketing Strategy.** Tenang AI markets primarily through its TikTok account (tenang.ai) and LinkedIn. The MIT Solve 2024 Global Health Challenge selection serves as a key trust signal. The company positions itself as the accessible, affordable, and stigma-free alternative to traditional counseling.

**Competitive Position.** Tenang AI competes at the prevention/triage layer rather than the treatment layer. Its AI-first model can scale without human resource constraints, unlike Riliv, KALM, and Bicarakan.id which depend on licensed psychologist networks. However, clinical effectiveness and user trust in AI-only mental health support remain unproven at scale. Tracxn score 30/100 [@tracxn2026].
