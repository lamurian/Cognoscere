## Summary

Bicarakan.id is a Jakarta-based mental health platform connecting users with licensed psychologists through both online and offline counseling. Founded by Andreas Handani and Matt, it was selected for the Startup Studio Indonesia batch 3 program by Kemenkominfo. East Ventures invested in Bicarakan.id as part of its 2021 mental health thesis alongside Riliv, Intellect, and Mindtera [@stacyoentoro2022; @kompascom2021].

## Key Points

**User Base.** As of November 2021, Bicarakan.id had served over 12,000 users during the COVID-19 pandemic, providing counseling to people experiencing stress, anxiety, depression, addiction, and relationship problems [@kompascom2021]. The company has 1,265 LinkedIn followers as of 2026.

**Funding.** Bicarakan.id was invested in by East Ventures in 2021 as part of the VC firm's mental health portfolio buildup. Exact funding amounts are undisclosed. The company also participated in the Startup Studio Indonesia accelerator (batch 3) run by Kemenkominfo [@stacyoentoro2022].

**Product Features.** Bicarakan.id offers: (1) Online counseling sessions with licensed psychologists (all practitioners hold SIPP -- Surat Izin Praktik Psikolog); (2) Offline face-to-face counseling through Rumah Bicara (a physical counseling center in Jakarta for individuals and couples); (3) Mental health content via blog and social media. The platform serves individuals, couples, and families [@kompascom2021].

**Go-to-Market Strategy.** Unlike Riliv's campus-first approach, Bicarakan.id launched as a B2C-centric platform focused on online counseling with an offline expansion arm (Rumah Bicara). The hybrid online-offline model differentiates it from purely digital competitors. Bicarakan.id's GTM emphasized affordable, quality mental health care to address the pandemic-driven surge in demand [@stacyoentoro2022; @kompascom2021].

**Current Marketing Strategy.** Bicarakan.id relies on government accelerator backing (Startup Studio Indonesia) for credibility, content marketing through psychology articles, presence on Google Play Store, and word-of-mouth from its offline Rumah Bicara location in Jakarta. The company has not pursued aggressive social media growth compared to Riliv's TikTok strategy.

**Competitive Position.** Bicarakan.id is less visible in public rankings than Riliv. Its hybrid model (online + offline physical clinic) is unique among Indonesian mental health startups, which are predominantly digital-only. However, this also limits scalability compared to fully digital competitors.
