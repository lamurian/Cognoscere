## Summary

Carbon offset quality depends on five metrics: realness, additionality, leakage, permanence, verification [@hoover2021]. Verra (Verified Carbon Standard) handles ~40% of voluntary market transactions, with protocols like VM0042 for agriculture. Other registries: American Carbon Registry, Climate Action Reserve, Gold Standard.

VM0042 requires project developers to demonstrate regulatory surplus, barrier analysis, and <20% adoption (common practice test) [@cariappa2024]. Third-party verifiers independently confirm protocol compliance. Credits are serialized and registered, then retired when sold to prevent double-counting.

For SMEs, aggregation through cooperatives or carbon market programs reduces per-unit costs. USDA programs (Growing Climate Solutions Act) aim to lower barriers through certification of verifiers and technical assistance providers [@hoover2021]. Carbon prices need to reach USD 40-80/tCO2e to incentivize widespread adoption [@cariappa2024].
