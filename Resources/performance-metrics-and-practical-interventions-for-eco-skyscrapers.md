Performance metrics for eco-skyscrapers include: energy use intensity (kWh/m2/year), green plot ratio (vegetated area as percentage of site area), operational carbon footprint, and biodiversity index (species count established). Bosco Verticale achieves a 10:1 green plot ratio — 30,000 m2 of vegetation on a 3,000 m2 site [@alkodmany2023]. Practical interventions proven at scale include: double-skin facades with integrated greenery (CapitaGreen reduces solar heat gain by 26%), vegetated exoskeletons (Oasia Downtown's 1,100% green plot ratio), staggered cantilevered balconies for tree planting (Bosco Verticale), automated shading systems inspired by Islamic mashrabiyya (Al Bahar Towers), on-site cogeneration and ice storage (Bank of America Tower), and wind turbine integration through building form (Pearl River Tower, Bahrain World Trade Center producing 15% of power) [@alkodmany2018; @alkodmany2023]. Modular planting systems and smart irrigation with sensor monitoring reduce maintenance requirements — a key challenge identified by practitioners.
