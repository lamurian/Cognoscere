BPS Statistics Indonesia publishes Weekly Retail Price Movement of Selected Staple Goods covering rice, cooking oil, beef, chicken, eggs, chili, shallots, and sugar across provincial capitals. The data is released as periodic PDF publications accessible at https://www.bps.go.id/. Prices are collected through the Consumer Price Survey conducted weekly across 90 Indonesian cities. This is the primary official source for tracking food price inflation at weekly frequency.
