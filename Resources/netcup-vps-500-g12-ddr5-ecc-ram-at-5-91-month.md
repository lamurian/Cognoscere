## Summary

Netcup VPS 500 G12 is a 12th-generation virtual server offering 2 dedicated vCores (x86), 4 GB DDR5 ECC RAM, and 128 GB NVMe storage. The price is €5.91/month inclusive of 19% German VAT (approximately €4.97/month or $5.40/month excluding VAT). It also supports hourly billing at €0.010/hour for flexible short-term use. DDR5 ECC RAM and the large 128 GB NVMe storage set this plan apart from competitors at this price point.

Netcup is a German hosting provider with a strong reputation in the European market. The VPS 500 G12 supports multiple data center locations (Vienna, Nuremberg, Amsterdam, Manassas/USA, Singapore), pre-configured OS images, DDoS protection, snapshot support with Copy-On-Write, and remote console access. A 12-month minimum contract term applies for the monthly rate, or you can use hourly billing with no minimum term.

The 2 dedicated vCores (as opposed to shared vCPUs) give more predictable CPU performance than shared-CPU alternatives, making this a stronger choice for workloads with consistent CPU demand. However, the dedicated vCores come at a slightly higher price than shared-CPU competitors, and the 12-month contract term is less flexible than Hetzner's no-minimum-term model.
