The ICT&health World Conference is an international event focused on healthcare innovation, artificial intelligence, and digital transformation. It draws healthcare professionals, IT leaders, researchers, and policymakers from across Europe and beyond. The conference includes keynotes, workshops, a trade exhibition, and B2B matchmaking sessions.

## Dates and Deadlines

- Conference dates: January 27-29, 2026
- B2B matchmaking event runs alongside the main conference
- Registration opens: typically months in advance via the ICT&health website

Call for speakers and presentation proposals are published through the ICT&health website preceding the event.

## Fees

Specific fee amounts for 2026 are not publicly listed on the website. Pricing typically varies by:

- Single-day vs full-conference passes
- Early bird vs standard rates
- Professional vs academic vs student categories

Fee waivers may be available for speakers, students, and academic researchers. The B2B matchmaking component may offer complimentary participation for qualifying attendees. Contact the organisers directly.
