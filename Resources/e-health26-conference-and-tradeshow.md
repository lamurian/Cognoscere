## Summary

e-Health26 is the premier Canadian event for digital health professionals, hosted by Canada Health Infoway, the Canadian Institute for Health Information (CIHI), and Digital Health Canada. Since 2000, it has attracted over 1,400 health professionals annually for education, networking, and trade show exhibits.

Abstract submissions went through a blind peer-review process. Accepted presentation formats include panel (30 min, 3 presenters), oral (20 min, up to 2 presenters), and e-poster (10 min, 1 presenter). One primary presenter per accepted submission receives a reduced-fee registration.

## Fees (CAD)

- Digital Health Canada Member: CA$1,456

- Emerging Professional (first 5 years of career): CA$730
- Patient/Patient Partner: CA$342

- Single day: CA$846

## Fee waivers

Speaker reduced-fee registration available for accepted presenters. Limited number of student and emerging professional discounted registrations. No visa invitation letters provided.

## Open to PhD students

Yes. Student registration available at CA$342 with proof of full-time enrolment required. Submissions were open to anyone working to improve Canadian healthcare, including researchers, clinicians, and patients.
