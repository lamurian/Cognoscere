ViVE is a curated digital health event jointly produced by HLTH and the College of Healthcare Information Management Executives (CHIME). It brings together C-suite executives, senior digital health leaders, health startups, investors, and policymakers to focus on the business of healthcare transformation.

## Dates and Deadlines

- Conference dates: February 22-25, 2026
- Welcome reception: Sunday evening, February 22, 2026
- Main programming: February 23-25, 2026
- Early bird price increase: January 9, 2026

This is an industry conference, not an academic call-for-papers event. Speaker proposals are reviewed through HLTH's speaker management process.

## Fees

- Standard pass: starting approximately $1,500-2,500 depending on registration timing
- Early bird pricing saves approximately $900+ vs standard rate
- Provider and Payer Connect program: complimentary tickets for qualifying purchasing decision-makers

Fee waivers are available through the Provider and Payer Connect program for qualifying healthcare providers and payer organisations. Startup discounts and group rates may also be available. Contact ViVE directly.
