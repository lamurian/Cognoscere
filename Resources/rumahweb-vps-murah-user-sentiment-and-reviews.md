## Overview

Rumahweb is one of Indonesia's oldest hosting providers, founded in 2002 in Yogyakarta with over 400,000 domains and 200,000 customers. User sentiment for their VPS Murah (cheap VPS) line is mixed — the provider is well-established but has significant negative reviews. Based on reviews from Trustpilot (2.7/10, 9 reviews), WHTOP (5.2/10, 19 reviews), HostAdvice (2.9 expert rating), and jokowa.id monitoring.

## What Users Love

**Dual availability zone deployment** is Rumahweb's unique value proposition. Users can deploy VPS across two separate data center locations (Zone A: Bogor Tier-3, Zone B: Bekasi Tier-4) for high-availability architecture.

**Free cPanel Solo** on mid-tier plans (M and above) is valued by users who prefer a GUI control panel. The promotion runs until November 2026.

**Free .CLOUD domain** with every VPS purchase is a small but appreciated bonus.

**Established reputation** — as one of Indonesia's oldest hosting companies, Rumahweb has name recognition and long-term stability that newer providers lack.

## The Major Pain Points

**1. Major 2021 Incident.** A significant outage in 2021 caused downtime for almost a month, with data loss reported. One HostAdvice reviewer stated: "Major incident at 2021 down for almost 1 month, without backup data or connection link." This has severely damaged trust despite the company being established.

**2. Low Trustpilot Score.** 2.7/10 on Trustpilot from 9 reviews. WHTOP user rating is 5.2/10 (19 reviews). These are the lowest scores among all budget Indonesia VPS providers in this comparison.

**3. Support Quality Decline.** A 2025 review noted: "CS Mengalami Penurunan yang Sangat Signifikan" (Customer service has experienced a very significant decline). Users report slower response times and less helpful support compared to earlier years.

**4. SATA SSD Only.** No NVMe option on the VPS Murah line. Users running database workloads report I/O bottlenecks.

**5. Port 25 Blocked by Default.** Users needing to send email from their VPS must request a manual whitelist, which adds friction.

## Verdict

Rumahweb's dual-zone HA capability and established brand are genuine strengths, but the 2021 outage and declining support quality raise serious concerns. For users who need multi-zone redundancy (a genuinely unique feature), Rumahweb may be worth considering with careful backup planning. For single-server production use, other providers offer better reliability and performance at similar prices. The Trustpilot and WHTOP scores are the lowest among all providers in this comparison.
