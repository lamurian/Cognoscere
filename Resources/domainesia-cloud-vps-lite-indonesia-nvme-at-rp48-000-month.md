- Higher-tier plans jump significantly in price.

## Strength and Limitations

- **Strength:** Excellent value at Rp48,000/month for a 1 GB NVMe VPS. RAID10 provides data redundancy at the storage level. Established provider with good support reputation. Application templates reduce setup time.
- **Limitation:** No sub-1 GB plan for ultra-budget needs. International bandwidth speed is not specified. Unmanaged only.

## Verdict

A strong mid-range option among budget Indonesia VPS providers. Rp48,000/month for 1 GB NVMe with RAID10 is competitive. DomaiNesia's established reputation and 24/7 human support make it a safer choice than smaller unnamed providers. Best for single-application deployments where 1 GB RAM is sufficient.
