Samuel Sekuritas Indonesia publishes a Weekly Economic Insights report authored by Chief Economist Dr. Fithra Faisal Hastiadi. It covers GDP outlook, inflation, trade balance, foreign exchange reserves, consumer confidence, monetary policy analysis, and geopolitical impacts on the Indonesian economy. Reports are available in both English and Indonesian as free PDF downloads at https://samuel.co.id/research-reports/samuel-weekly-economic-insights/. This is the most detailed free weekly macro analysis from the private sector.
