## Summary

Hetzner CX23 is the entry-level shared vCPU cloud server in Hetzner's Cost-Optimized line. It delivers 2 vCPU cores (Intel/AMD x86_64), 4 GB RAM, 40 GB NVMe SSD, and 20 TB included traffic per month. Pricing starts at €3.49/month in German and Finnish data centers, rising to ~€5.49/month in US/Singapore regions, excluding VAT. There is no minimum contract term — billing is hourly with a monthly price cap.

This is the cheapest option globally that meets the 4 GB RAM requirement with a reputable provider. Hetzner operates data centers in Germany, Finland, the US (Ashburn, Hillsboro), and Singapore, with GDPR compliance, 99.9% uptime SLA, 24/7 ticket support, and ISO 27001 certification. The CX23 uses shared vCPUs, meaning CPU performance shares hardware with neighboring VMs, making it ideal for low-to-medium CPU usage workloads like web servers, small databases, VPNs, and development environments.

## Key Points

- **Provider:** Hetzner Cloud (hetzner.com)
- **Plan:** CX23 (Cost-Optimized, shared vCPU)
- **URL:** https://www.hetzner.com/cloud/cost-optimized
- **vCPU:** 2 shared (Intel Xeon/AMD EPYC, x86_64)
- **RAM:** 4 GB DDR4 ECC
- **Storage:** 40 GB NVMe SSD
- **Traffic:** 20 TB/month included
- **Network:** 10 Gbit infrastructure, 1 IPv4 address, IPv6 /64 subnet
- **Price:** €3.49-5.49/month depending on region (excl. VAT). EU regions ~€3.49-3.99/month ≈ $3.80-4.35. After June 2026 price adjustment, CX23 replaced the earlier CX22 at similar pricing.
- **Contract:** No minimum term. Hourly billing with monthly cap. Pay only for hours used if deleted mid-month.
- **Add-ons:** Snapshots (€0.01/GB/month), backups (from €0.10/instance/month), floating IPs (€0.50/month), block storage (€0.04/GB/month)
- **Locations:** Falkenstein (Germany), Nuremberg (Germany), Helsinki (Finland), Ashburn (US), Hillsboro (US), Singapore
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime guarantee
- **Support:** 24/7 ticket support
- **Limitations:** Shared vCPU — not suitable for sustained high CPU loads. Ticket-only support (no live chat). No managed databases included. IPv4 address pricing is included, but additional IPv4 costs €0.50/month.
