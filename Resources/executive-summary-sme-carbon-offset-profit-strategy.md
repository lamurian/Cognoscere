## Summary

SMEs can profit from carbon offsets through land-based practices. Carbon pricing shows a leverage effect of 1.78% emission reduction per 1% price increase [@song2025]. The voluntary market transacted ~100 MMTCO2e in 2018 with forestry at 37% of volume [@hoover2021].

Four scenarios evaluated: (1) Conservation Agriculture — USD 18-30/ha/season, low capital [@cariappa2024]; (2) Agroforestry — higher sequestration, longer payback; (3) Smart IMTA-Aquaponics — USD 3,572/20m2/cycle, 46.98% ROE, solar adds USD 100-200/cycle credits [@goda2025]; (4) Integrated — combines long-term sequestration with immediate cash flow.

**Highest profit density:** Smart IMTA-aquaponics (46.98% ROE). **Most accessible:** Conservation agriculture on existing farmland. **Best value:** Integrated agroforestry-aquaponics.
