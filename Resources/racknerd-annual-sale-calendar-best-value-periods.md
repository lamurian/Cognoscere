## Summary

RackNerd runs annual promotional sales on a predictable calendar. The best value-for-price period is **Black Friday / Cyber Monday (late November)**, when the 4 GB KVM VPS drops to $29.98/year ($2.50/month). The second-best is the **New Year sale (January)**, when the same plan is $43.88/year ($3.66/month). Between these two events, the entire period from Black Friday through Chinese New Year (late November to late January/February) is a continuous sale season with overlapping deals. Outside these seasonal events, RackNerd maintains a permanent "Specials" page with year-round promotional pricing at $59.99/year ($5.00/month) for the 4 GB plan.

The 4 GB KVM VPS plan (the one of interest at $59.99/year baseline) follows this discount ladder across sale events:

| Sale Event | Typical Timing | 4GB Price | Saving vs Baseline |
|------------|---------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Year-round specials | Always available | $59.99/yr ($5.00/mo) | Baseline |
| New Year sale | January | $43.88/yr ($3.66/mo) | 27% off |
| Black Friday / Cyber Monday | Late November | $29.98/yr ($2.50/mo) | 50% off |

Each sale event includes the lifetime price lock — the promotional price you pay is the price you renew at forever, as long as you renew before expiry.

## Key Points

### Black Friday / Cyber Monday (Best — Late November)

- RackNerd's biggest annual event. Historically runs from mid-to-late November, often announced around Nov 19-28.
- **4 GB KVM VPS at $29.98/year** (BF 2025 pricing: 3 vCPU, 65 GB SSD, 6.5 TB bandwidth)
- Includes the **Wheel of Fortune** feature — spin for extra discounts and perks on top of already-reduced pricing
- **Free double bandwidth**: post your order number on the LowEndTalk thread and RackNerd doubles your monthly bandwidth at no cost
- Major giveaways (MSI gaming laptops, Samsung QLED TVs, Synology NAS, RackNerd credit, merchandise)
- New hardware nodes and datacenter locations often launched alongside the event
- Inventory can be limited — popular locations sell out fast

### New Year Sale (Good — January)

- Runs from late December through January (historically available through mid-to-late January)
- **4 GB KVM VPS at $43.88/year** (New Year 2026 pricing: 3 vCPU, 105 GB SSD, 9 TB bandwidth)
- Slightly better specs per tier than Black Friday (e.g., 105 GB vs 65 GB SSD at the 4 GB tier)
- Los Angeles DC-02 (Asia-optimized) is typically restocked during this sale
- Less competitive than Black Friday, so less sell-out pressure on locations

### Continuous Sale Season (Black Friday to Chinese New Year)

- Reddit community reports note the promotional period runs **"Black Friday through Christmas and New Year, right up until Chinese New Year"** (typically late January to mid-February)
- Overlapping promotions mean you can often buy at New Year pricing even in late December
- After Chinese New Year, sales return to baseline year-round pricing

### Year-Round Specials (Baseline — Always Available)

- The "Specials" page at https://www.racknerd.com/specials/ maintains continuous promotional pricing
- **4 GB KVM VPS at $59.99/year**: 3 vCPU, 60 GB SSD, 7 TB transfer, 1 Gbps port
- This is the baseline to compare against seasonal events
- Still excellent value at $5.00/month with lifetime price lock

### Strategy Recommendations

- **If you can wait until late November:** Black Friday is the absolute best value. The 4 GB plan at $29.98/year is half the year-round price with better specs (larger SSD and more bandwidth at BF 2025).
- **If you need the VPS sooner:** New Year deals in January at $43.88/year are still 27% below baseline. Buy during this window and you lock in that price for life.
- **If you need it now:** The year-round $59.99/year plan on the Specials page is still the cheapest 4 GB VPS under $6/month with lifetime price lock. No need to wait — lock in the price now and you can always buy another at Black Friday pricing later.
- **Stacking strategy:** Buy your primary VPS at the year-round price now, then add a second VPS at Black Friday pricing in November for isolation (staging, CI/CD, or backup).

### Important Notes

- All promotional prices include lifetime price lock — the sale price applies at every renewal, not just the first term
- No standard refunds (case-by-case only) — test within the first week
- Black Friday inventory is limited — order early in the event
- You can early-renew at any time (Generate Renewal Invoice button in client portal)
- Server hardware on promotional plans uses older Intel Xeon E5 generations; NVMe is only on the higher-cost Ryzen VPS line
