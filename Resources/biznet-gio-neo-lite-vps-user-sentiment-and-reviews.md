## Overview

Biznet Gio is the cloud computing division of Biznet Networks, one of Indonesia's largest ISPs with its own fibre backbone. The NEO Lite VPS line generates the most positive user sentiment among all budget Indonesia VPS providers. Based on reviews from tasikhost, fazar.net benchmark, kangserver benchmark, YouTube reviews, and WPTR (4,577 score), and CekIPSaya.

## What Users Love

**Network quality is the standout feature.** Multiple reviewers consistently report excellent stability and low latency. As one reviewer stated: "ping sering tidak stabil, kadang koneksi putus-putus... setelah pindah ke Biznet Gio VPS, semua masalah tersebut hilang" (After switching to Biznet Gio, all those problems disappeared). The fact that Biznet owns its fibre backbone means local connectivity is best-in-class.

**Customer support responsiveness** is frequently praised. One notable example: a user requested a PTR record update via email and received a response in under 10 minutes. This level of responsiveness is unusual for budget VPS providers.

**Uptime reliability** is well-documented. A public uptime status page (stats.uptimerobot.com/KZl1jp2VSS) allows users to verify the 99.9% SLA independently. The fazar.net review confirmed no unplanned downtime during a 30-day test, with only scheduled maintenance notified days in advance.

## The Major Pain Points

**1. Standard SSD on Base Tier.** The NEO Lite XS 1.1 uses standard SSD, not NVMe. Benchmark results show 4k IOPS around 1,000 (read/write), which is moderate. One reviewer noted: "IOPS pada blok kecil hasilnya cukup rendah" (IOPS on small blocks is quite low). Users running database-heavy workloads should upgrade to NEO Lite Pro (AMD EPYC + NVMe).

**2. CPU Performance.** The base NEO Lite uses a common KVM processor clocked at ~2.0 GHz (real 1.9 GHz). CPU-bound tasks show modest performance. One commenter noted: "CPU nya aja yg masih 2GHz (real 1,9GHz)."

**3. International Bandwidth.** While local speed tests show excellent results (559 Mbps down, 889 Mbps up on speedtest.net), international throughput is significantly lower. The fazar.net benchmark showed London at 36 Mbps send, Los Angeles at 32 Mbps send.

**4. Mixed Reviews on Google.** Some Google reviews report dissatisfaction with service and slow VPS performance. One reviewer noted: "di beberapa review google, ada yg kurang puas sama pelayanannya dan VPS nya lemot katanya." However, this was countered by other users suggesting configuration issues.

## Verdict

Biznet Gio has the strongest network quality and the most passionate user advocates among Indonesia VPS providers. For users who prioritise network stability and local latency above all else, it is the clear winner. The base NEO Lite tier is best suited for general web serving and lightweight applications. Database-heavy or CPU-intensive workloads should use NEO Lite Pro. As one reviewer concluded: "Bagi siapa pun yang sedang mencari VPS handal di Indonesia, Biznet Gio adalah pilihan yang sangat saya rekomendasikan."
