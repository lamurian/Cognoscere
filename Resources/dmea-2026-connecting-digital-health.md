DMEA is Europe's leading event for digital health, integrating trade fair, congress, academy, and networking. The 2026 edition runs under the theme "Time to Connect Digital Health" with over 900 exhibitors and 470 speakers.

## Dates and Deadlines

- Conference and exhibition: April 21-23, 2026
- Registration deadline for exhibitors: July 1, 2025
- Call for papers/proposals: Typically opens in the preceding year and closes several months before the event

DMEA accepts speaker and workshop proposals through an open call published on its website months in advance.

## Fees

- Exhibition space: from €275/m² (corner stand area €280/m²)
- Day tickets for trade fair and congress: available through the online ticket shop
- Full congress passes: pricing varies by category (professional, academic, student)

Fee waivers are not generally available. Some partner organisations (e.g., LIOF Netherlands) offer free day tickets for pre-arranged B2B meetings. Student discounts may be available.
