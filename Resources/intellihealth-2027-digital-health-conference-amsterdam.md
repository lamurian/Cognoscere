## Summary

IntelliHealth 2027 is organised by The Science and Information (SAI) Organization, bringing together healthcare providers, academia, life sciences, and government leaders. The conference focuses on six transformation pillars including AI in healthcare, digital health equity, precision medicine, wearable monitoring, and smart health systems. It is held alongside IntelliSys in Amsterdam.

Abstract submission is free with no submission or review fee. Authors can choose between oral or poster presentation formats. Accepted abstracts are published online with DOI and included in the official IntelliHealth 2027 Book of Abstracts.

## Conference Details

- **Conference title**: IntelliHealth 2027 - Advancing Intelligence in Healthcare
- **Date**: May 20-21, 2027
- **Location**: Park Plaza Amsterdam Airport, Melbournestraat 1, 1175 RM Lijnden, Amsterdam, Netherlands

## Deadlines

Early Round 1:
- Abstract submission deadline: February 1, 2026
- Acceptance notification: March 1, 2026
- Registration deadline: April 1, 2026

Early Round 2:
- Abstract submission deadline: April 1, 2026
- Acceptance notification: May 1, 2026
- Registration deadline: June 1, 2026

Regular Round:
- Abstract submission deadline: July 1, 2026
- Acceptance notification: August 1, 2026
- Registration deadline: September 1, 2026

All rounds lead to the conference on May 20-21, 2027.

## Fees

- Early Round 1: €399
- Early Round 2: €599
- Regular Round: €799

Fee includes access to all stages, medical expo floor, event app, networking areas, social program, lunches, and refreshments.

## Fee waivers

No refund or waiver policy beyond possible press passes for accredited journalists. No student rate specified. Abstract submission is free.

## Open to PhD students

Yes. The call for abstracts is open to researchers from academia, and the themes cover AI, digital health, robotics, and related fields relevant to PhD research.
