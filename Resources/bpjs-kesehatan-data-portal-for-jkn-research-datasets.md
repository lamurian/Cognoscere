BPJS Kesehatan, Indonesia's National Health Insurance (JKN) administrator, maintains a data portal at `data.bpjs-kesehatan.go.id` offering 75 datasets on the JKN program. These include sample membership data, anonymised claims records (Data Sampel BPJS Kesehatan 2015-2022), membership demographics by segment (PBI subsidised, non-PBI, employment-based), healthcare facility utilisation rates, and catastrophic disease claims distribution.

The portal uses a custom web interface (not CKAN) and does not offer a documented public API. Data access is primarily through the web portal's dataset viewer and downloadable files (XLSX, CSV, PDF). Some pages require login or may be geo-restricted to Indonesian IPs based on community monitoring reports. The portal's availability has been intermittent.

For researchers, the JKN Sample Dataset (2015-2022) is the most valuable asset — it provides anonymised individual-level data suitable for epidemiological analysis, health systems research, and equity assessments of Indonesia's universal health coverage program. Aggregate statistical reports are published as articles and visualisations alongside the raw datasets.
