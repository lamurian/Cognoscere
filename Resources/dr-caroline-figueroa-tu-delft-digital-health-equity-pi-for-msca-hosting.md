## Summary

Dr. Caroline Figueroa is Assistant Professor in Digital Health at Delft University of Technology (TU Delft) and leads the RISE Digital Health research group. She is a medical doctor by background with a PhD in neuroscience of depression from the University of Amsterdam and Oxford [@figueroa2026]. Her research centers on developing, testing, and implementing personalized digital health tools for underserved populations, including ethnic minorities and lower socioeconomic groups. While she does not currently have an Indonesia-specific project, her focus on digital health equity and implementation science aligns well with rural Indonesia digital health research.

## Key Details

- **Institution**: Faculty of Technology, Policy and Management, TU Delft
- **Role**: Assistant Professor in Digital Health, Lead of RISE Digital Health group
- **Field**: Digital health, implementation science, personalized interventions, mental health, health equity, participatory design
- **Email**: c.figueroa@tudelft.nl
