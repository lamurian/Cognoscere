Bank Indonesia publishes the Jakarta Interbank Spot Dollar Rate (JISDOR) as the official daily USD/IDR reference rate. The rate is published each weekday at https://www.bi.go.id/en/statistik/informasi-kurs/jisdor/ and is freely accessible. JISDOR enables daily tracking of rupiah movements for monitoring currency stability, import and export cost pressures, and foreign investor sentiment toward Indonesia. Historical data back to 2021 is available on the same page. This is the most direct publicly accessible weekly-frequency indicator of Indonesia's external position.
