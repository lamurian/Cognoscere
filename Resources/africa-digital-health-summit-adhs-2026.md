## Summary

The Africa Digital Health Summit (ADHS) 2026 is an Africa-focused conference with the theme "From Pilots to Scale: Growing Africa's Digital Health Innovation Ecosystem." It targets professionals, researchers, and policymakers working on digital health transformation across the African continent. The summit includes both oral and poster presentations selected from peer-reviewed abstracts.

Accepted abstracts are presented at the conference and published in the conference proceedings. The summit is organised by Premier Health Systems (PHS) Consult.

## Conference Details

- **Conference title**: Africa Digital Health Summit (ADHS) 2026
- **Date**: June 25-26, 2026
- **Location**: Abuja Continental Hotel, Wuse, Abuja, Federal Capital Territory, Nigeria

## Deadlines

- **Abstract submission deadline**: February 27, 2026 (CLOSED)
- **Acceptance notification**: March 27, 2026
- **Full presentation submission deadline**: June 6, 2026
- **Conference dates**: June 25-26, 2026

Abstract requirements: maximum 300 words, structured as Title, Authors, Contact, Introduction, Objectives, Methods, Results, Conclusions. Maximum 5-6 keywords.
