The Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) publishes weekly market statistics covering the Jakarta Composite Index (JCI), sectoral indices, trading volume and frequency, market capitalization, and foreign net buy and sell values. Daily statistics are also published each trading day. Access at https://www.idx.co.id/en/market-data/statistical-reports/statistics/. The foreign flow data is particularly useful for measuring foreign investor sentiment toward Indonesia on a weekly basis.
