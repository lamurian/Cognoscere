HIMSS26 is the annual Global Health Conference & Exhibition organised by the Healthcare Information and Management Systems Society (HIMSS). It is one of the largest and most influential gatherings for healthcare leaders driving digital transformation.

## Dates and Deadlines

- Conference dates: March 9-12, 2026 (pre-conference forums on March 9)
- Main exhibition and sessions: March 10-12, 2026
- Early bird registration deadline: December 14, 2025
- Advance registration deadline: Not publicly specified

This is an industry conference, not an academic call-for-papers event. However, HIMSS accepts speaker proposals months in advance through its speaker management system.

## Fees

Pricing is tiered by registration timing and membership status:

- HIMSS member early bird: approximately $1,000-1,500
- HIMSS member standard: approximately $1,500-2,000
- Non-member early bird: approximately $1,500-2,000
- Non-member standard: approximately $2,000-2,500

Promo code HL726 saves $250 off registration. Add-on preconference forums carry additional fees.

Fee waivers are not generally available. HIMSS offers group discounts and member rates through partner organisations (NACCHO, TECHNATION, etc.).
