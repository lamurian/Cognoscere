- Sign:
  - [Tachycardia](Resources/tachycardia.md) 
  - Belly swelling
  - Fever
- Symptoms:
  - Belly pain
  - Diarrhea, could be bloody
  - Dizziness
  - Confusion
- On abdominal physical examination:
  - Guarding
  - Rebound tenderness
  - Percussion tenderness
