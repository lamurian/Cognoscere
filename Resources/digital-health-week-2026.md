Digital Health Week 2026 is a collaborative academic conference hosted by the University of Sydney, the University of New South Wales, the University of Queensland, and the University of Melbourne. The theme is "Digital Futures: Rethinking Health and Care". It is run exclusively in-person across three Australian locations.

## Dates and Deadlines

- Conference dates: February 3-5, 2026
- Day 1-2 (Feb 3-4): Face to face at multiple sites
- Day 3 (Feb 5): Face to face at University of Melbourne and University of Sydney
- Abstract submission deadline: Extended to February 15, 2026
- Original abstract deadline: Not specified

Abstracts are invited for conference presentations and ePosters covering AI, data analytics, remote monitoring, virtual care, and social media in healthcare. Late submissions are not accepted.

## Fees

Specific registration fee amounts are not publicly listed on the conference website. As a university-hosted academic conference, fees are typically modest compared to industry events. Contact the organisers at digital.health-week@sydney.edu.au for current pricing.

Fee waivers may be available for students, early-career researchers, or presenters from low-income institutions. Contact the organising committee for inquiries.

- University of Melbourne, Parkville VIC 3010, Australia
