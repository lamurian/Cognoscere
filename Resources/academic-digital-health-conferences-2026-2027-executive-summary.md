Unlike industry trade shows, these conferences feature peer-reviewed abstract selection and academic publication venues. Conferences are ordered by upcoming date.

## Key Observations

**Best option for PhD students**: APRU Global Health Conference 2026 in Hong Kong. Abstract submission is open until July 31, 2026. Student rate is US$175. The conference has a dedicated Digital Health Transformation track and all accepted abstracts are published in the Journal of Public Health and Emergency (JPHE), indexed in Web of Science and Scopus. The Student Poster Contest awards US$500 per category winner.

**Most affordable**: eHealth2026 in Helsinki at €95 for students, though the development project abstract deadline (June 9) has just closed.

**Most recent deadline still open**: APRU Global Health Conference 2026 (abstracts due July 31, 2026) and IntelliHealth 2027 (regular round due July 1, 2026, conference in May 2027).

**Peer-reviewed publication**: APRU GHC 2026 publishes in JPHE (indexed). eHealth2026 best abstracts are invited for the Finnish Journal of eHealth and eWelfare.

**Geographic spread**: North America (Canada), Africa (Nigeria), Europe (Finland, Netherlands), and Asia (Hong Kong) are all represented.
