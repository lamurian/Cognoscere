## Summary

Prof. Matthias Rieger is Professor of Global Health at Erasmus School of Health Policy & Management (ESHPM), Erasmus University Rotterdam, appointed December 2024. He is a development economist working on economic and behavioural determinants of health and nutrition outcomes, primarily using RCTs and field experiments [@rieger2025]. He currently has field experiments on school meal vouchers for adolescents in Indonesia. He chairs the Global Health Theme at ESHPM, which focuses on Global Health Behaviours & Socio-Economic Determinants of Health, alongside International Health Care Systems (led by Igna Bonfrer).

He is setting up a behavioural economics lab to design and test scalable health/nutrition interventions. This lab model could accommodate an MSCA fellow working on digital health implementation.
