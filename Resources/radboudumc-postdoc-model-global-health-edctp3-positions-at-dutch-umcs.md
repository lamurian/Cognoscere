## Summary

The Postdoctoral Researcher in Infectious Disease Modelling at Radboudumc (deadline 12 July 2026, now closed) illustrates the typical salary range, requirements, and structure of global health postdoc positions at Dutch university medical centres. This TIMERA project position was funded by Global Health EDCTP3, focused on epidemic preparedness and modelling capacity strengthening in sub-Saharan Africa. It serves as a model for the type of positions the user should monitor.

Key structural features include: 1-year initial contract with possible extension, travel to LMICs for face-to-face training, integration with international consortium, and supervision of fellows. The salary range (EUR 3,724-5,867/month) is standard for UMC postdocs and increases by 3.5% in July 2026 and another 3.5% in May 2027 under the collective labour agreement.

## Key Points

- **URL**: https://www.academictransfer.com/en/jobs/362084/ (closed but illustrative)
- **Salary**: EUR 3,724-5,867/month (scale 10) + 8% holiday allowance + 8.3% end-of-year bonus
- **Requirements**: PhD in epidemiology, biostatistics, public health, data science, or related quantitative field; R/Python; willingness to travel to LMICs
- **Description**: Applied disease modelling research; teaching development; fellow supervision; evidence translation for public health
- **Travel to Indonesia**: Not included in this specific post (Africa-focused) but analogous positions could involve Indonesia
- **Remote Work**: Partial home-working compensated (EUR 2.40/day)
- **Approach**: Search academictransfer.com and euraxess.ec.europa.eu for global health postdocs at Dutch UMCs
