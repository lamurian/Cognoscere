SLM-Bench, accepted at EMNLP 2025, is the first benchmark systemically assessing SLMs across accuracy, computational efficiency, and environmental sustainability. It evaluates 15 SLMs on 9 NLP tasks using 23 datasets spanning 14 domains, on 4 hardware configurations, quantifying 11 metrics across correctness, computation, and energy consumption [@pham2025].

**Key findings.** The results reveal diverse trade-offs among SLMs: some models excel in accuracy while others achieve superior energy efficiency. No single model dominates all dimensions. SLM-Bench provides an open-source pipeline with standardized evaluation protocols, enabling fair comparisons under controlled hardware conditions. This framework bridges the gap between resource efficiency and real-world applicability for SLM deployment decisions.
