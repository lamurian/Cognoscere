## Summary

The 19th APRU Global Health Conference 2026 is hosted by the JC School of Public Health and Primary Care at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). The conference theme is "Resilience and Adaptation: Navigating the Evolving Landscape of Global Health in a Changing World." It includes a dedicated Digital Health Transformation sub-theme, making it directly relevant to digital health researchers.

The conference accepts abstract submissions for either oral or poster presentations. All accepted abstracts will be published in the Journal of Public Health and Emergency (JPHE), indexed in Web of Science (ESCI) and Scopus. Selected authors are invited to develop full papers for a special journal edition with publication fees waived.

## Conference Details

- **Conference title**: 19th APRU Global Health Conference 2026
- **Date**: December 7-9, 2026
- **Location**: Henry Cheng International Conference Centre, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong

## Deadlines

- **Abstract submission deadline**: July 31, 2026 (OPEN)
- **Acceptance notification**: Mid-August to August 31, 2026
- **Early bird registration deadline**: September 30, 2026
- **Conference dates**: December 7-9, 2026

## Fees

Early bird rates (on or before September 30, 2026):
- International participants: HK$2,740 (US$350)
- Low- and Lower-Middle-Income Countries (LLMICs): HK$1,760 (US$225)
- Students: HK$1,370 (US$175)

Regular rates (from October 1, 2026):
- International: HK$3,370 (US$430)
- LLMICs: HK$2,200 (US$280)
- Students: HK$1,700 (US$215)

## Fee waivers

Reduced rates for students and LLMIC delegates. The Student Poster Contest awards US$500 cash prize per category winner. Full-time undergraduate and graduate students (excluding postdoctoral researchers) are eligible.

## Open to PhD students

Yes. Abstract submissions welcome from researchers, practitioners, policymakers, and students worldwide. Student Poster Contest open to full-time students.
