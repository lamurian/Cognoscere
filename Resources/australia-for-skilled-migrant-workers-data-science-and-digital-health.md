## Summary

Australia recruits skilled migrants through the points-based General Skilled Migration program (Subclass 189/190) and the Global Talent Visa (Subclass 858). Data scientist and digital health roles are on the skilled occupation list. Major hubs are Sydney and Melbourne, with strong demand from CSIRO AEHRC, the Australian Digital Health Agency, Atlassian, Canva, and major hospital networks [@pandey2026]. Regular openings: data scientist, digital health consultant, FHIR interoperability specialist, clinical informatics lead, and health AI researcher. Australia is investing heavily in national digital health infrastructure under its National Digital Health Strategy.

## Key Details
Free public education for children through primary and secondary school. Medicare provides universal healthcare access for permanent residents, covering GP visits and public hospital treatment. PhD data scientist salary: AUD 110,000 - AUD 175,000 (mid-level average ~AUD 130,000, senior specialised roles reach AUD 175,000+) [@yosifova2025; @codelabsacademy2025]. Living cost: AUD 3,500 - 4,500/month for a single person including rent. Numbeo estimates AUD 1,594/month excluding rent; city-centre one-bedroom rent averages AUD 2,200. Sydney is most expensive; Brisbane and Adelaide offer lower costs [@numbeo2026; @numbeo2026e].
