## Summary

Prof. Constance Schultsz is a medical doctor, medical microbiologist, and Professor of Global Health (Emerging Infectious Diseases and Antibiotic Resistance) at Amsterdam UMC, University of Amsterdam. She is Director of Science and Chair of the Board at AIGHD (Amsterdam Institute for Global Health and Development) [@schultsz2026]. She leads the SPIN project on AMR surveillance in Bandung (Java) and Medan (Sumatra), Indonesia. She has supervised PhD students and postdocs, and her group currently includes multiple postdoctoral scientists.

## Key Details

- **Institution**: Amsterdam UMC (Department of Global Health) & AIGHD
- **Role**: Professor of Global Health, Deputy Head of Global Health, Director of Science at AIGHD
- **Field**: Antimicrobial resistance, molecular epidemiology, zoonotic diseases, AMR surveillance, global health
- **Email**: c.schultsz@amsterdamumc.nl / c.schultsz@aighd.org
- **MSCA suitability**: High — has coordinated EU consortia (JPI-AMR HECTOR), chairs Public Private Partnership AMR-Global, has multiple postdocs in her group
