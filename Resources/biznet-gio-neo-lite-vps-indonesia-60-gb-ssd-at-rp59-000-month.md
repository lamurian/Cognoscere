## Pros

- 60 GB SSD at the base plan is the most storage among all cheapest-tier options.
- Biznet owns its fibre backbone — excellent local connectivity and low latency within Indonesia.
- Unlimited bandwidth with no FUP.
- 10 Gbps network port (shared).
- Established provider (Biznet is a major Indonesian ISP).
- Additional disk and snapshot features available.

## Cons

- Slightly more expensive than competitors at Rp59,000/month.
- Standard SSD, not NVMe, on the base NEO Lite tier.
- Unmanaged only.
- No 512 MB RAM option.
- International bandwidth may be congested during peak hours.

## Strength and Limitations

- **Strength:** Biznet owns the network infrastructure end-to-end. 60 GB SSD base storage is generous. Unlimited bandwidth on a 10 Gbps port. Good for storage-heavy applications within Indonesia.
- **Limitation:** Standard SSD is slower than NVMe competitors at similar prices. No sub-Rp50,000 tier. International routing is not Biznet's strength.

## Verdict

Best choice if you need generous storage (60 GB) at the entry level and value local network quality. Biznet's infrastructure ownership means reliable connectivity within Indonesia. The NEO Lite Pro (AMD EPYC / NVMe) is worth the upgrade for production workloads. Rp59,000/month for 60 GB SSD is the best storage-to-price ratio among all budget Indonesia VPS options.
