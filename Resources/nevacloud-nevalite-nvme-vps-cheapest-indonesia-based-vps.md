## Overview

Nevacloud offers the Nevalite NVMe line, the cheapest paid VPS with servers physically located in Jakarta, Indonesia. It runs on Intel Xeon Platinum processors with SSD NVMe RAID10 storage and unlimited bandwidth. The provider also offers a higher-tier Nevacloud NVMe line using AMD EPYC Genoa processors with 3x replicated distributed storage.

## Ease of Use, Maintenance, and Configuration

- Dashboard-based management with one-click OS reinstall.
- Full root access via SSH.
- Pay-as-you-go (per-hour) and fixed-term (monthly) billing available.
- No control panel included (unmanaged). Optional cPanel/Plesk can be added.
- Automated snapshot support for backups.
- 24/7 support via ticket and WhatsApp.

## Pros

- Cheapest Indonesia-based VPS at Rp42,240/month (~$2.76). No other provider beats this price.
- Unlimited bandwidth with no FUP throttle on local Indonesian traffic.
- NVMe SSD RAID10 storage — fast I/O for database workloads.
- Separate Nevalite (budget) and Nevacloud (premium AMD EPYC) tiers let you scale.
- Per-hour billing available for dev/test workloads.

## Cons

- Unmanaged — you handle all OS and application maintenance.
- Nevalite line uses Skylake-era Xeon Platinum, not the latest generation.
- International bandwidth may be limited compared to local traffic.
- Smaller provider — less community documentation than bigger names.
- No Windows VPS option on the cheapest tier.

## Strength and Limitations

- **Strength:** Unbeatable price-to-performance ratio for Indonesia-hosted VPS. NVMe RAID10 at this price point is rare. Suitable for staging, dev servers, low-traffic production, and learning.
- **Limitation:** Unmanaged only, older CPU architecture on the budget tier, and limited international bandwidth capacity. Not ideal for latency-sensitive international users.

## Verdict

The cheapest paid VPS with Indonesia-based servers. At Rp42,240/month, it is hard to beat for budget-constrained projects that need local Indonesian latency. Use Nevalite for staging and dev; step up to the AMD EPYC tier for production.
