## Summary

eHealth2026 is the 31st annual conference organised by the Finnish Society of Telemedicine and eHealth (STeHS) in collaboration with the Finnish Society for Effectiveness in Social and Healthcare. The theme is "Effective digital solutions in health care and social welfare services." The conference targets healthcare professionals, researchers, developers, and decision-makers in social services, healthcare, rehabilitation, and technology.

The call for scientific abstracts closed earlier, but development project abstracts (paper posters not undergoing formal peer review) were accepted until June 9, 2026. The conference has sessions in both English and Finnish, but all abstracts must be submitted in English.

## Conference Details

- **Conference title**: eHealth2026 - International Conference on eHealth and Digital Health
- **Date**: October 1-2, 2026
- **Location**: University of Helsinki Main Building, Helsinki, Finland

## Deadlines

- **Scientific abstract submission**: Closed (deadline May 3, 2026)
- **Development project abstract submission**: Closed (deadline June 9, 2026)
- **Early bird registration deadline**: June 30, 2026
- **Conference dates**: October 1-2, 2026

## Fees

Early bird price (valid until June 30, 2026):
- Members of Finnish Effectiveness Society or STeHS: €420 (+ VAT 25.5%)
- Non-members: €450 (+ VAT 25.5%)
- Undergraduate students: €95 (+ VAT 25.5%)

From July 1, 2026:
- All participants: €525 (+ VAT 25.5%)

## Fee waivers

Undergraduate student rate is heavily discounted at €95. The early bird fee is applicable for all accepted abstract authors. No explicit fee waiver program beyond student discount.

## Open to PhD students

Yes. The conference welcomes researchers and the abstract submission was open to all. The student rate applies to undergraduate students; PhD students likely pay the member/non-member rate.

Accepted abstracts are published in the Conference Book, and the best abstracts are invited for full papers in the Finnish Journal of eHealth and eWelfare.
