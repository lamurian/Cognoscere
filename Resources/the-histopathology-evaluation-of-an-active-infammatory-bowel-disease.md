- Infiltration of the lamina propria, composed of:
  - Neutrophils
  - Macrophages
  - Dendritic cells
  - Natural killer T cells
- Increased level of:
  - Tumor necrosis factor-a (TNF-a)
  - Interleukin-1b
  - Interferon-gamma
  - Cytokines of the interleukins-23-TH17 pathway
