## Pros

- NVMe storage at entry level (20 GB).
- Free backup image included with all plans — good for disaster recovery.
- 10 Gbps local bandwidth with direct peering to Indonesian IXPs (OpenIXP, IIX-APJII, JKT-IX).
- Easy-to-use VPS panel with IPMI-level control.
- 10+ years in business.
- No minimum contract.

## Cons

- More expensive entry point at Rp75,000/month compared to competitors.
- International bandwidth is limited to 200 Mbps shared.
- Not permitted for VPN/Proxy/Tunnel use per TOS.
- Unmanaged only.
- Prices exclude 11% VAT.
- Uptime SLA is 99.5% (lower than the 99.9% offered by competitors).

## Strength and Limitations

- **Strength:** Built-in free backup image is a differentiator — most competitors either charge extra or don't offer automated restores. NVMe storage on all plans. Good local peering for Indonesian traffic.
- **Limitation:** Higher price and lower SLA (99.5%). TOS restrictions on VPN/proxy use limit deployment options. The 200 Mbps international cap may affect global users.

## Verdict

A reliable mid-range option if you value built-in backup capability and NVMe storage. The free backup image feature is unique among budget Indonesia VPS providers. However, the higher price (Rp75,000/month vs Rp42,000-59,000 from competitors) and TOS restrictions on VPN use make it less flexible. Best for standard web hosting and application serving within Indonesia.
