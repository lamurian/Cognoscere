## Overview

Nevacloud is a relatively new Indonesian cloud provider (established ~2023) offering budget NVMe VPS. User sentiment is cautiously positive. The provider is most discussed on Indonesian tech blogs, comparison sites, and Instagram developer communities. Based on reviews from Goodfirms (4.7/10, 10 reviews), VPSMetrics benchmarks, and Indonesian tech blogs (alfasoleh, arifsetiawan, naqiibah).

## What Users Love

The NVMe RAID10 storage at this price point is the most frequently praised feature. Users running database-backed applications report good random I/O performance. The unlimited bandwidth with no FUP on local Indonesian traffic is consistently valued. Indonesian-language support via WhatsApp and chat is appreciated by local users — "tim support berbahasa Indonesia yang responsif banget" (Indonesian-speaking support team that is very responsive).

The pay-as-you-go per-hour billing option is highlighted as useful for dev/test workloads. Several Indonesian developers on Instagram have collaborated with Nevacloud for VPS credit sponsorships at coding events, suggesting community engagement.

## The Major Pain Points

**1. Newer Provider, Limited Track Record.** Nevacloud has only been operating for ~2-3 years. Users looking for long-term stability are cautious. No independent long-term uptime monitoring data is publicly available.

**2. No Published SLA.** Unlike DomaiNesia (99.9%) or Biznet Gio (99.9%), Nevacloud does not publish a Service Level Agreement. This is a concern for production use.

**3. Older CPU on Budget Tier.** The Nevalite line uses Intel Xeon Platinum Skylake (2017-era). Users running CPU-intensive workloads report modest performance. The benchmark results from VPSMetrics confirm moderate single-core performance.

**4. Limited International Bandwidth.** While local traffic is unlimited and fast, international bandwidth appears to be shared and capped at lower speeds. Users serving global audiences report slower throughput.

**5. Small Community.** Fewer community-written tutorials and troubleshooting resources compared to established providers.

## Sentiment by Platform

- **Goodfirms:** 4.7/10 rating from 10 reviews. "Reliable and user-friendly Cloud VPS in Indonesia." modest score suggests room for improvement.
- **Indonesian Tech Blogs (alfasoleh, arifsetiawan):** Positive comparisons against Vultr, Contabo, RackNerd. Consistently recommend for local Indonesian use. "Untuk penggunaan di Indonesia, Nevacloud menawarkan kombinasi latency rendah, kecepatan stabil, dan uptime yang konsisten."
- **VPSMetrics benchmarks:** No user reviews but benchmark data confirms NVMe performance is competitive at this price tier.
- **Instagram/Tech Communities:** Active engagement with developer community through VPS credit sponsorships. Positive brand perception among student developers.

## Verdict

Nevacloud has the strongest price-to-performance ratio among budget Indonesia VPS providers, but the lack of a published SLA and the provider's young age make it a better fit for staging, dev, and non-critical workloads than production. The NVMe RAID10 storage at Rp42,000/month is genuinely impressive. As one reviewer put it: "Nevacloud lebih unggul dari sisi value" (Nevacloud is superior in terms of value).
