## Summary

Ireland provides the fastest EU permanent residency pathway via the Critical Skills Employment Permit (CSEP), granting Stamp 4 after 2 years. Data scientist, ICT professional, and digital health roles are on the Critical Skills Occupations List. Major hubs are Dublin (tech/pharma HQ hub), Cork (pharma and medtech), and Galway (medtech). The HSE is actively investing in digital health under its Digital Health Strategic Implementation Roadmap [@pandey2026]. Regular openings: data scientist, health data analyst, FHIR/health interoperability specialist, digital health consultant, and clinical informatics specialist.

## Key Details
Free primary and secondary education for children. Public healthcare (HSE) provides universal hospital services; GP visit and medical cards available based on income. Private health insurance is common. PhD data scientist salary: EUR 60,000 - EUR 120,000 (typical PhD range EUR 65,000-90,000, senior roles at multinationals reach EUR 100,000+) [@pandey2026; @codelabsacademy2025]. Living cost: EUR 2,200 - 3,000/month including rent. Numbeo estimates EUR 1,008/month excluding rent; city-centre one-bedroom rent averages EUR 1,571, higher in Dublin (EUR 2,000-2,500). Cork and Galway are 15-20% cheaper than Dublin [@numbeo2026d; @numbeo2026e].
