## Summary

Smart IMTA-FRS (Floating Raft System) generates USD 3,572 revenue per 180-day cycle from a 20m2 footprint, with 46.98% Return on Equity compared to 19.14% for traditional soil culture [@goda2025]. Aquatic biomass reaches 999.49 kg/cycle (57% higher than conventional) and vegetable yield 1,554.95 kg/cycle (84% higher).

Solar power reduces energy costs dramatically: USD 0.003/kWh LCOE vs. USD 0.029/kWh grid power [@goda2025]. The 3kW solar system costs USD 870 and powers pumps and aerators. IMTA-FRS smart remains profitable even with 30% cost increases, demonstrating exceptional financial resilience.

Initial investment (USD 4,236 smart vs. USD 2,123 conventional) is recouped through higher yields and lower operating costs. Smart sensors and IoT monitoring increase yield by reducing FCR (1.62 conventional vs. 0.70 smart system-wide) and optimizing growing conditions [@goda2025].
