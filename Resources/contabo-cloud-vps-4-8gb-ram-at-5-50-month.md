## Summary

Contabo Cloud VPS 4 offers the most RAM-per-dollar of any option researched: 4 vCPU cores, 8 GB RAM, 100 GB SSD storage, and unlimited traffic on a 200 Mbit/s port. The price is €5.50/month (approximately $6.00), with a promotional rate of €4.40/month for the first 24 months. With 8 GB RAM at this price, it provides 2x the RAM of all other options at a similar or minimally higher cost.

Contabo is a German hosting provider established in 2003, serving 225,000+ customers across 190 countries. They offer data centers in Germany (Munich, Nuremberg), the US (St. Louis), and Singapore. The Cloud VPS 4 includes free DDoS protection, a configurable firewall, dedicated IPv4 and IPv6, unlimited traffic, and a Customer Control Panel for management. One-click apps (Docker, n8n, Nextcloud, WireGuard) and custom ISO deployment are supported.

The key trade-off is CPU performance: Contabo uses older hardware generations with shared resources, and the 200 Mbit/s port speed is significantly slower than Hetzner's 10 Gbit or RackNerd's 1 Gbit infrastructure. User reviews and benchmarks consistently note that Contabo's CPU performance lags behind spec sheets under sustained load. For bursty, low-CPU workloads (web servers, file storage, development), the generous RAM allocation is a clear advantage. For CPU-intensive tasks, the slow port speed and shared CPU become bottlenecks.

- **Provider:** Contabo (contabo.com)
- **Plan:** Cloud VPS 4 (Core VPS line)

- **Locations:** Germany (Munich, Nuremberg), US (St. Louis), Singapore
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime guarantee
- **Support:** 24/7 ticket support, phone support available

SSD storage, not NVMe (NVMe is on the Performance VPS tier at higher cost). Unlimited traffic subject to fair usage throttle for abusive usage patterns.
