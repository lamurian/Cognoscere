PEFINDO, Indonesia's domestic credit rating agency, publishes the Weekly Economic Update (WEU) every Monday. The report covers global and domestic macroeconomic developments, commodity markets, stock markets, exchange rates, and government securities auction results. It synthesizes multiple official data releases into a single analysis document. Free PDF download at https://www.pefindo.com/weekly-economic-update. This is the most accessible weekly synthesis report for tracking Indonesia's economic situation.
