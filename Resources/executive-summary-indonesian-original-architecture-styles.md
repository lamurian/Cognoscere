This research examined why Indonesian original architecture styles are significant and how they manifest. All questions from the research plan were addressed.

**WHY question (significance).** Indonesian rumah adat embody profound cosmology linking earth and sky, social structures, and community identity. They demonstrate sophisticated climate and material adaptation using stilt construction, steep roofs, natural materials, and passive cooling. Each ethnic group expresses unique identity through distinct architectural forms. Confidence: HIGH (supported by multiple peer-reviewed sources [@widayati2019; @rahim2023; @khumaira2025]).

**HOW question (manifestation).** Construction uses mortise-and-tenon joints, wooden pegs, and flexible systems that absorb earthquakes. Regional variations emerge from different social structures and cosmologies: Joglo/Limasan in Java, Rumah Gadang in Minangkabau, Tongkonan in Toraja, and Balinese compounds with Hindu cosmology. Preservation faces challenges from economic pressures and loss of crafts knowledge. Confidence: HIGH [@wikipediacontributors2025; @anggakara2020; @pardo2023].

**Key supporting questions addressed:** cultural and historical values, local material and climate influences, ethnic identity reflection, construction techniques, regional design variations, and preservation/modernisation balance. All 8 questions from the research plan were answered with sourced evidence. Conflicting evidence was checked but no significant contradictions were found in the literature.
