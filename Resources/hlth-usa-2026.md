HLTH USA is the leading healthcare innovation event in the United States. The 2026 edition brings together over 12,000 healthcare leaders including providers, payers, employers, investors, startups, and policymakers to discuss AI, policy, investment, and care delivery.

## Dates and Deadlines

- Conference dates: November 15-18, 2026
- Registration: opens typically mid-year
- Speaker proposals: accepted through HLTH speaker portal months in advance

This is an industry conference, not an academic call-for-papers event. No paper or abstract submission process.

## Fees

- Clinician pass: starting at $1,500
- Standard industry pass: approximately $2,000-3,000 depending on timing
- Startup passes and group rates available

Fee waivers may be available for clinicians, healthcare providers, and qualifying startups. HLTH offers a limited number of complimentary passes for buyers and decision-makers through its hosted buyer program. Contact HLTH directly.
