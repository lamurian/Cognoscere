The Directorate General of Budget Financing and Risk Management (DJPPR) of Indonesia's Ministry of Finance publishes weekly Government Securities (SUN) auction results every Tuesday. The reports include total bids received, amount awarded, bid-to-cover ratio, and weighted average yields across multiple bond series. Access at https://djppr.kemenkeu.go.id/. Auction results signal government borrowing costs, market demand for Indonesian sovereign debt, and investor confidence in fiscal policy.
