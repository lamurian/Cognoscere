## Overview

Rumahweb is an established Indonesian hosting provider offering VPS Murah (cheap VPS) with servers in two availability zones: Zone A at TechnoVillage Bogor (Tier-3 DC) and Zone B at DCI Bekasi (Tier-4 DC). The cheapest plan starts at Rp50,000/month for 1 vCPU, 512 MB RAM, and 20 GB SSD with unlimited traffic.

## VPS Specs (Cheapest Plan — Linux 512 MB)

- **vCPU:** 1 Core
- **RAM:** 512 MB
- **Storage:** 20 GB SSD
- **Bandwidth:** Unlimited
- **Network:** 1 Gbps backend network
- **Virtualisation:** KVM
- **Data Centers:** Zone A — TechnoVillage Bogor (Tier-3), Zone B — DCI Bekasi (Tier-4, +Rp10,000/month)
- **IP:** Dedicated IPv4
- **DDoS Protection:** Included
- **Dynamic Firewall:** Centralised firewall with dynamic rulesets

## Ease of Use, Maintenance, and Configuration

- Full root access.
- OS options: AlmaLinux, CloudLinux, Debian (8-12), Ubuntu (16.04-24.04).
- Optional cPanel (Solo/Admin/Pro/Plus/Premier) and Plesk licenses available.
- Free cPanel Solo on plans M and above (until November 2026).
- Free .CLOUD domain with every VPS purchase.
- Semi-managed support if using cPanel.
- 24/7 support via live chat, phone, and ticket.
- Automated backups twice per week (provider-managed only, not user-accessible).

## Pros

- Two availability zones for HA deployments across separate data centers.
- Tier-4 data center option (DCI Bekasi) for higher reliability.
- Free .CLOUD domain included.
- Free cPanel Solo on mid-tier and above.
- DDoS protection and dynamic firewall included.
- Multiple internet backbone connections with local exchange peering (IIX, OpenIXP, CDIX).
- Established provider since early 2000s.

## Cons

- 512 MB RAM is very limited for modern applications.
- SATA SSD, not NVMe.
- Backup is provider-managed and not user-accessible.
- Port 25 blocked by default (must request whitelist for email).
- No refunds after activation.
- Outdated OS support for older Debian/Ubuntu versions.

## Strength and Limitations

- **Strength:** Two data center zones separated by ~50 km enable proper HA architecture. Tier-4 option available. Free cPanel on mid-tier plans reduces management overhead. Good choice if you need cross-zone redundancy.
- **Limitation:** 512 MB RAM at entry level is too small for most production uses. No NVMe. The realistic minimum for usable production is Rp60,000/month (1 GB plan).

## Verdict

A solid option if you need multi-zone HA or Tier-4 data center reliability. For a single production VPS, Rp60,000/month for 1 GB RAM is reasonable. The free cPanel Solo on higher plans adds significant value for users who prefer a GUI control panel. Not the cheapest, but the dual-zone feature is unique among budget Indonesia VPS providers.
