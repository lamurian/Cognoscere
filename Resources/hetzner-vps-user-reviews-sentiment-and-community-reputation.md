## Summary

Hetzner enjoys among the strongest reputations in the Linux sysadmin and self-hosting communities for raw price-performance, but user sentiment on review platforms is sharply polarised. On Trustpilot, Hetzner scores 3.1/5 from 2,858 reviews (51% 5-star, 38% 1-star). On HostAdvice, the score is 2.8/5 from 317 user reviews. This split reflects a core tension: technical users who value performance and pricing love Hetzner; non-technical users or those who hit account-fraud-detection issues often hate it.

## What Users Love

**Price-performance ratio.** This is the dominant theme across all positive reviews. Users consistently say Hetzner offers 30-50% lower pricing than DigitalOcean, Vultr or Linode for equivalent or better specs. The 20 TB included traffic (vs 2-4 TB at competitors) is frequently highlighted. On VPSBenchmarks, Hetzner's consistency score is 71/100, and independent monitors record 99.96-99.99% uptime.

**Excellent hardware.** AMD EPYC-Genoa on the CPX tier and Ampere Altra on the CAX tier deliver strong CPU performance. NVMe RAID-10 storage benchmarks above 1 GB/s sequential reads. The 10 Gbit network infrastructure is praised.

**Responsive support (when it works).** Positive reviews report ticket resolution within minutes to hours for technical issues. Dedicated server users praise KVM remote-hand support and extended access during troubleshooting.

**GDPR compliance.** German jurisdiction, ISO 27001 certification, and no exposure to US CLOUD Act or FISA are cited as advantages for privacy-conscious users.

## Major Pain Points

**Aggressive account fraud screening.** The most common 1-star complaint. Accounts are rejected, suspended or abruptly terminated with canned responses citing "irregularities" or "concerns" about customer information. Users report losing access to 5+ running servers with no data recovery option. Hetzner's standard reply: "we do not close accounts randomly."

**Price increases.** The April 2026 adjustment raised prices 30-37% across several tiers. While Hetzner states this applies only to new orders, some users report unexpected charges on existing services. The wording on rescaling triggering new pricing is a source of confusion.

**Ticket-only support, no live chat.** Users with urgent issues report 3-10 day email response times, paid international phone support with long hold times, and calls being dropped. There is no live chat. Hetzner's support operates business hours (Mon-Fri 8am-6pm CET), which is problematic for timezone-offset users.

**Aggressive billing and account lockout.** Multiple users report full account suspension (including all paid services) for a single missed payment on an unrelated service. No grace period, no escalated warnings before lockdown. Data is permanently deleted after extended non-payment despite users attempting to pay.

**No refunds for unused service.** Users report Hetzner refusing refunds even when the service never worked correctly, or when billing errors occurred due to the provider's own system.

## Sentiment Breakdown by Community

- **r/selfhosted and r/hetzner:** Predominantly positive. "Solid hoster," "best value on the market," "I trust it with my work." Highly recommended for Linux-savvy users.
- **r/VPS:** More cautious. Multiple threads warn about account termination risk and aggressive fraud detection. "Hetzner: a good VPS option or a dangerous trap for small businesses?"
- **Trustpilot/HostAdvice:** Bimodal. Satisfied users give 5/5 for value and performance. Dissatisfied users give 1/5 for account/support issues.

## Verdict

Hetzner is excellent for technical users who pass account verification and never miss a payment. The performance and pricing are genuinely best-in-class. However, the aggressive fraud detection, ticket-only support, and zero-tolerance billing policies make it risky for non-technical users or businesses that cannot tolerate sudden account loss. The GitHub competitor comparison summarises it well: "Hetzner for production workloads where you want reliable performance at a reasonable price."

## Key Points

- Trustpilot: 3.1/5 from 2,858 reviews (51% 5-star, 38% 1-star)
- HostAdvice: 2.8/5 from 317 user reviews
- Uptime: 99.96-99.99% from independent monitors
- VPSBenchmarks consistency: 71/100
- Main praise: price-performance, NVMe, 20 TB traffic, 10 Gbit network
- Main complaints: fraud-detection account closures, ticket-only support with slow responses, aggressive billing lockout, 30-37% price increase in April 2026
- Best for: Linux-savvy developers, self-hosters, European-focused projects
- Avoid if: you need hand-holding support, live chat, or cannot tolerate risk of sudden account termination
