Under the IMF Special Data Dissemination Standard (SDDS), Bank Indonesia publishes weekly International Reserves and Foreign Currency Liquidity (IRFCL) data. The methodology has been consistent since March 2003 for weekly data. The dataset covers official reserve assets, foreign currency reserves, IMF reserve position, SDRs, and gold. Access the data at https://www.bi.go.id/en/statistik/sdds/ where it is downloadable in Excel format. This enables weekly tracking of Indonesia's external buffer adequacy.
