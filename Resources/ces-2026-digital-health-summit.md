The Digital Health Summit is a premier programme track within CES, the world's largest consumer technology event. It covers health technology innovations including AI diagnostics, wearable devices, telemedicine, and remote patient monitoring. CES 2026 hosted over 15,000 industry attendees listing digital health as an interest area.

## Dates and Deadlines

- CES 2026: January 6-9, 2026
- Digital Health Summit sessions: January 6-9, 2026 (integrated into CES schedule)
- Registration opens: typically by mid-year of preceding year
- Early bird deadline: typically October/November preceding year

This is an industry event, not an academic conference. No paper or abstract submission process. Companies apply for exhibition space and speaking opportunities through CES channels.

## Fees

CES registration fees vary by timing and category (2025 reference pricing):

- Early bird rate: approximately $500-800
- Standard rate: approximately $900-1,200
- On-site rate: approximately $1,500-1,700
- Digital Health Summit content is included in the CES registration fee

Fee waivers are not generally available for CES. Student rates and media passes are offered at reduced pricing. Startups may qualify for discounted exhibition packages through CES Eureka Park.
