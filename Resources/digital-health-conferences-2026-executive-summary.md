## Key Observations

**Most academic/scientific**: Digital Health Week 2026 (Australia, call for abstracts with peer review) and ICT&health World Conference 2026 (Netherlands, academic and professional mix) are the closest to legitimate scientific conferences with formal submission processes.

**Most affordable entry**: CES Digital Health Summit (included in CES registration from $500 early bird) and DMEA (day tickets available, exhibition space from €275/m²).

**Most expensive**: Health.Tech Global Summit (CHF 2,500+) and HLTH USA ($1,500-$3,000).

**Waiver possibility**: ViVE offers complimentary passes through its Provider and Payer Connect program. Some conferences have student rates or discounts for qualifying participants.

**Las Vegas concentration**: Three major conferences (CES, HIMSS26, HLTH USA) are in Las Vegas, making it the single most active city for digital health conferences in 2026.
