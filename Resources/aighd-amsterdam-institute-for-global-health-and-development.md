## Summary

The Amsterdam Institute for Global Health and Development (AIGHD), based at Amsterdam UMC, conducts interdisciplinary research on antimicrobial resistance, chronic care, health economics, infectious disease elimination, and urbanisation and health [@aighd2026]. AIGHD coordinates EU-funded digital health projects (EDCTP3) for pandemic preparedness in LMICs and uses digital health tools for disease diagnosis. In July 2026, AIGHD relocated to Amsterdam UMC, securing long-term sustainability.

AIGHD regularly recruits researchers through Amsterdam UMC and University of Amsterdam. Its LMIC research focus and digital health tools expertise make it a natural host for digital health implementation research in Indonesia.

## Key Points

- **URL**: https://www.aighd.org
- **Salary**: Standard UMC postdoc EUR 3,724-5,867/month; includes holiday allowance (8%) and end-of-year bonus (8.3%)
- **Requirements**: PhD in global health, epidemiology, health economics, digital health, or related field; LMIC experience
- **Description**: Global health research institute with digital health tools focus; EDCTP3 projects; pandemic preparedness and disease diagnosis in LMICs
- **Travel to Indonesia**: Potentially — works across multiple LMICs including Southeast Asia
- **Remote Work**: Partial depending on role
- **Approach**: Monitor Amsterdam UMC/UvA vacancies; contact AIGHD about MSCA fellowship hosting
