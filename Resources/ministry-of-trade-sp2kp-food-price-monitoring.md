The Ministry of Trade runs SP2KP (Sistem Pemantauan Pasar dan Kebutuhan Pokok), a daily real-time price tracking system for essential food commodities. It monitors 10+ items including rice, cooking oil, sugar, beef, chicken, eggs, chili, shallots, and garlic with national and provincial average prices. The web portal at https://sp2kp.kemendag.go.id/ provides interactive maps and price comparison tables. This is the highest-frequency official source for tracking food price pressures in Indonesia.
