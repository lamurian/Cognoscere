# Areas

## Purpose

Areas hold notes about ongoing responsibilities. These are not tasks with a deadline. They are parts of life you maintain over time.

Examples: health, career, finance, mathematics knowledge, parenting, personal growth.

## What belongs here

- Subjects you want to keep expertise in (e.g. linear algebra, statistics).
- Career paths, roles, and industries you track.
- Domains of continuous learning (e.g. mental health, health economics).
- Standards, principles, or frameworks you refer back to.

## What does not belong

- One-off projects with a specific goal. Put those in **Projects**.
- Reference material without personal stake. Put those in **Resources**.
- Inactive content. Move to **Archives**.

## Agent usage

When searching or writing in Areas, consider:

- Is the topic an ongoing responsibility, not a finite task?
- Does the user need to maintain or deepen knowledge here?
- Prefer linking to other notes using `[[wikilink]]` or markdown links.

## File convention

`kebab-case-title.md` with YAML frontmatter (`title`, `date`, `tags`, `author`, `source`).
